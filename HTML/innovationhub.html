Pilikula Regional Science Centre

Pilikula Regional Science Centre (PRSC) is a public science museum located in Mangaluru, Karnataka, India. It is part of the larger Pilikula Nisargadhama, a multi-purpose eco-education and tourism complex managed by the District Administration of Dakshina Kannada.

Location

PRSC is situated at Moodushedde, approximately 9 km from Mangaluru city, and is accessible via road, rail, and air. It is nestled within a 10-acre campus surrounded by lush greenery.

Establishment

The centre was developed jointly by:

  • Department of Science & Technology, Government of Karnataka
  • National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), Ministry of Culture, Government of India

It was inaugurated in 2014 as part of a national initiative to promote science education and public engagement.

Educational Role

PRSC conducts regular science demonstrations, teacher training programs, student workshops, and mobile science exhibitions. It plays a vital role in STEM outreach across the region.

Integration with Pilikula Nisargadhama

PRSC is part of the broader Pilikula Nisargadhama, which includes:

  • Biological park
  • Botanical garden and arboretum
  • Heritage village
